Bird Trap 3000

ACME MFG Co. — "Your friends in birdwatching!"

Materials

• Barrel

• Rope (100 ft)

• Pulley

• ACME Dehydrated Electricity

• ACME Inflatable Swimming Pool

Instructions

1. Inflate and fill ACME Inflatable Swimming Pool, placing under a large overhanging rock.

2. Attach pulley to the rock, over the pool, and thread the rope through.

3. Tie the rope to the barrel and fill with half a jar of ACME Dehydrated Electricity.

4. Hoist the barrel above the pool.

5. Wait for the bird to take a bath, then drop the barrel for a shocking capture!
